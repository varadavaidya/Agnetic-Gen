Lionel Messi

Forward • Inter Miami CF & Argentina
Ballon d’Or (multiple) FIFA World Cup Winner Record Goal Scorer

Quick Facts

  • Full name: Lionel Andrés Messi
  • Born: 24 June 1987, Rosario, Argentina
  • Position: Right/central forward, playmaker
  • Clubs: Barcelona → Paris Saint-Germain → Inter Miami CF

Highlights

  • World Cup champion with Argentina (2022)
  • Multiple UEFA Champions League titles (Barcelona)
  • All-time top scorer for Barcelona & Argentina
  • Renowned for dribbling, vision, and playmaking

Style of Play

Messi is known for tight ball control, acceleration, and decisive passing in the final third. He often drops deep to create chances, combining as a playmaker and finisher.
